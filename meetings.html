The Faculty Senate typically meets at 6:30 pm, on the first Tuesday of every month during the academic year. Occasionally, extraordinary meetings of the Senate are convened. Meetings are typically held on Zoom, and sometimes in DeBartolo Hall. All Faculty Senate meetings are open to the public, except when the Senate is meeting in Executive Session.

Meeting minutes are provided online (below) after the meeting in which they are approved. For minutes from previous years, please visit our archive of meeting minutes.
